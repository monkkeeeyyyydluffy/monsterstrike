Gacha Lab

Gacha lab is new system for getting monthly pull that replaced the card flips. energy generates over time and maxes out at 30/30 roughly every 10hours.
so one way to maximize is to collect 3 times a day, morning, midday and night.
Need 300 energy of an element to reveal your pick.
Can reveal 10 (12 if monpass) picks and you can then choose from these picks for free pull.
UPDATED starting May. below mechanic will start at 500 cumulative energy, instead of 1000. Its been extremely difficult(maybe impossible) to get 5 picks of one element, so this is a welcome change.
Also has mechanic that once you collect 1000 cumulative energy, you generate more (rare) energy, and again at 2000.
