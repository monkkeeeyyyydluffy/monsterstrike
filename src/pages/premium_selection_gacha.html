Premium selection gacha

Premium selection gacha allows you to pick 6 monsters, and then ONLY THESE 6 monsters will appear when you roll a 5-6*.
The limited is 0.6% rate, genpools at 2.4% rate. This is one of the best chances to grab a specific genpool unit.
Can review GW pages for recommendations.

For my youtube video covering the picks, go here: Youtube premium selection gacha
